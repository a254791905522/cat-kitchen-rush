Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

Cat Kitchen Rush is an offline single-player iOS game. This policy explains how the app handles privacy.

Information Collection

Cat Kitchen Rush does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, settings, scores, level state, and star results locally on your device. This local data is used only to provide game functionality and is not sent to us.

Network, Ads, and Tracking

  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No analytics SDKs are included.
  • No tracking SDKs are included.
  • No social login, account system, or in-app purchase SDKs are included.
  • The game does not require an internet connection to play.

Children's Privacy

The game is designed as a family-friendly casual game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the app changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, please use the contact options on the App Store product page.
